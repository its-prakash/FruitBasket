Our Story & Mission

Our Journey

At FruitBasket, our mission is to make fresh, healthy fruits accessible to everyone. We believe that everyone deserves the best fruits without compromise. We started with a small team and a big dream.

What Drives Us

We're driven by a desire to make a positive impact. Healthy eating starts with access to fresh, wholesome fruits sourced from trusted farmers. We are motivated by the joy our customers feel when receiving fresh fruit baskets—making lives healthier, one delivery at a time.

Key Milestones

  • Launched FruitBasket with a loyal base of happy returning customers.
  • Expanded operations across various cities and towns.
  • Built strong, sustainable relationships with local farmers and suppliers.
